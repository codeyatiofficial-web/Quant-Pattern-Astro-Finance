The Algo Reads Greeks. You Do Not Have To.

Most traders pick options by price alone. Our algo picks options by intelligence.

△

Delta — Direction Confidence

Before buying any CE or PE our algo checks Delta. We only enter options where Delta is between 0.40 and 0.60. This means you are trading ATM options with maximum sensitivity to Nifty direction. Too low Delta means lottery tickets. Too high means expensive ITM premium. We find the sweet spot automatically on every single trade.

Γ

Gamma — Acceleration Check

Gamma tells us how fast Delta is changing. High Gamma near expiry means your option can double or go to zero very fast. Our algo monitors Gamma exposure and avoids entering trades when Gamma risk is too high. This protects you from the Thursday expiry Gamma explosion that destroys retail traders every single week.

Θ

Theta — Time Decay Guardian

Every option loses value every day due to Theta decay. The algo calculates exactly how much premium you are losing per hour and only enters trades where the expected move justifies the Theta cost. If Theta is eating your premium faster than Nifty can move — the algo simply does not trade that day. Capital protection comes first always.

V

Vega — Volatility Intelligence

Vega measures how much your option price changes when IV moves. Before every trade our algo checks IV Rank. If IV is above 70th percentile options are expensive and Vega risk is high — algo avoids buying. If IV is below 30th percentile options are cheap — algo prefers to buy. You get the best premium value on every trade automatically.
